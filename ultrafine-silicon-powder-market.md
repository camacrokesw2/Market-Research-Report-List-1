<p><ul><li>Densified Silicon Powder</li><li>Semi Densified Silicon Powder</li><li>Undensified Silicon Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrafine silicon powder market is categorized into three main types based on density. Densified silicon powder has a compact structure, enhancing its stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Semi densified silicon powder strikes a balance between density and porosity, offering versatility for various industrial uses. Undensified silicon powder, being the least compact, is typically used in applications requiring high surface area for reactions, such as in battery technology and chemical processes.</p></p>
