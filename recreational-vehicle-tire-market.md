<p><p>The Recreational Vehicle (RV) tire market features three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires, filled with air, offer better cushioning and traction, ideal for varied terrains. Solid tires, manufactured from solid rubber, provide durability and eliminate the risk of punctures, making them suitable for rugged use. Polyurethane tires blend the benefits of both, offering lightweight and wear-resistant characteristics while maintaining some air-filled efficiency. Each type caters to different RV needs, impacting performance and safety.</p></p>
