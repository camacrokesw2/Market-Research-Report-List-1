<p><strong>The LNG Loading & Offloading Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CBM (Conventional Buoy Mooring)</li><li>SPM (Single Point Mooring)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LNG loading and offloading systems encompass different mooring technologies, primarily CBM and SPM. CBM (Conventional Buoy Mooring) systems involve fixed structures that allow ships to securely moor and transfer LNG in a controlled manner. In contrast, SPM (Single Point Mooring) systems utilize a swiveling buoy, enabling vessels to safely moor while accommodating varying weather conditions. Both systems are crucial for efficient and safe LNG transport, catering to different operational needs and environmental conditions in the maritime industry.</p></p>
