<p><strong>The Jigsaw Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>T-Shank</li><li>U-Shank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jigsaw blades market is primarily categorized into T-shank and U-shank types, each designed for specific applications. T-shank blades feature a tang that fits securely into the jigsaw, providing stability and compatibility with most models, making them popular for precision cutting. U-shank blades, characterized by a U-shaped mount, are typically used in older jigsaw models and are easier to change. The choice between them often depends on the user's equipment and cutting needs, influencing market demand and trends.</p></p>
