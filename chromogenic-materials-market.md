<p><p>The chromogenic materials market comprises three primary types: photochromic, thermochromic, and electrochromic materials. Photochromic materials change color in response to light exposure, commonly used in sunglasses and windows. Thermochromic materials alter color with temperature changes, finding applications in mood rings and temperature-sensitive packaging. Electrochromic materials shift color when an electric charge is applied, used in smart windows and displays. Each type offers unique functionalities suited for diverse industries, enhancing aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency.</p></p>
