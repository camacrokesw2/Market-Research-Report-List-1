<p><p>The car shock absorber market is categorized by structure and control method. By control method, hydraulic shock absorbers use fluid dynamics for damping, suitable for most vehicles, while pneumatic shock absorbers utilize air pressure, offering adjustable firmness ideal for performance or off-road applications.</p></p>
