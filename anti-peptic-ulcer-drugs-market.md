<p><p>The Anti-Peptic Ulcer Drugs market encompasses various categories aimed at treating and preventing peptic ulcers. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) reduce stomach acid production, offering effective relief. Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB) provide an alternative mechanism for acid inhibition. Antacids neutralize existing stomach acid for immediate relief. H2 Antagonists block histamine receptors, decreasing acid secretion. Ulcer Protective Drugs create a protective barrier, promoting mucosal healing. Each type differs in mechanism and application, catering to diverse patient needs and treatment strategies.</p></p>
