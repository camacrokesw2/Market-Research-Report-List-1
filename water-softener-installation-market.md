<p><p>The water softener installation market comprises two main types: whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home water softeners treat all water entering a residence, ensuring soft water is available for all household uses, which is ideal for homes with hard water issues affecting plumbing and appliances. Split systems, on the other hand, focus on specific areas or points of use, enabling targeted treatment, often in smaller spaces or for specific applications like drinking or cooking water.</p></p>
