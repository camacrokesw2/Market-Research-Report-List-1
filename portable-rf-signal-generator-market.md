<p><p>The portable RF signal generator market is primarily divided into two segments: analog signal generators and vector signal generators. Analog signal generators produce continuous waveforms and are typically used for basic testing and maintenance tasks. Vector signal generators, on the other hand, generate complex modulated waveforms, enabling detailed analysis and testing of communication systems. They are essential for applications involving digital modulation techniques and advanced performance evaluations, making them vital for modern wireless technology development.</p></p>
