<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment</li><li>Powered Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Power Over Ethernet (PoE) market consists of Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) and Powered Devices (PD). PSE includes devices like PoE switches and injectors that supply power through Ethernet cables. They enable simpler network installations without additional power lines. Powered Devices, on the other hand, are those that receive power via the Ethernet connection, such as IP cameras, wireless access points, and VoIP phones. Together, they enhance efficiency and reduce infrastructure costs in industrial settings.</p></p>
