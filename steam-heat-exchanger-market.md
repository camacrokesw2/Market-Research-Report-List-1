<p><strong>The Steam Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heating Equipment</li><li>Indirect Heating Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam heat exchanger market is categorized into two main types: direct heating equipment and indirect heating equipment. Direct heating equipment involves the direct transfer of heat from steam to the heating medium, typically using steam coils or heating jackets, allowing for efficient heat transfer. In contrast, indirect heating equipment utilizes a heat exchanger to separate the steam from the heating medium, thereby preventing contamination and allowing for greater control over temperature. Both types serve critical roles across various industrial applications.</p></p>
