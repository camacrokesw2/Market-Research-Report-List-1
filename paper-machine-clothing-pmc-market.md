<p><p>Paper Machine Clothing (PMC) encompasses essential textile components used in the paper manufacturing process. The market can be divided into three main types: Forming Fabrics, which support the initial formation of paper sheets; Press Felts, which aid in the removal of water during pressing, enhancing sheet density; and Dryer Fabrics, which facilitate moisture removal during the drying phase. Each type is designed for specific functions, contributing to efficiency, quality, and productivity in paper production processes.</p></p>
