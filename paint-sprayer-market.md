<p><p>The paint sprayer market includes several key types: airless paint sprayers, HVLP (High Volume Low Pressure) paint sprayers, and others. Airless paint sprayers use high pressure to atomize paint, allowing for quick coverage of large surfaces with a smooth finish. HVLP sprayers operate at lower pressure, producing fine atomization, making them ideal for detailed work and reducing overspray. "Others" encompass various specialized sprayers like pneumatic or brush sprayers, catering to niche applications in both professional and DIY markets.</p></p>
