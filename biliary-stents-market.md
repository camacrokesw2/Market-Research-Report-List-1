<p><p>The biliary stents market comprises two main types: metal stents and plastic stents. Metal stents, often made of stainless steel or nitinol, are designed for long-term use, offering durability and resistance to collapse, making them suitable for patients with malignant biliary obstruction. Plastic stents, typically made from polyethylene, are more cost-effective and easier to place but are generally intended for short-term use, as they can become blocked more readily. Both types serve to relieve biliary obstruction and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
