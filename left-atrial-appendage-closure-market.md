<p><p>The Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Closure market comprises two main types: endocardial and epicardial devices. Endocardial LAA devices are placed inside the heart via catheter-based techniques, providing a minimally invasive approach for preventing stroke in patients with atrial fibrillation. In contrast, epicardial devices are positioned on the outer surface of the heart through surgical procedures, offering a more direct method for LAA occlusion. Both types aim to reduce thrombus formation and associated stroke risks while catering to different patient needs.</p></p>
