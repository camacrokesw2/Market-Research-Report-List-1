<p><p>The liner board market consists of three main types: Kraft liner, Kraft faced paper, and ordinary liner. Kraft liner is made from recycled pulp and is known for its strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty packaging. Kraft faced paper features a Kraft liner with a paper facing, providing a smoother finish for printing, often used for retail packaging. Ordinary liner is typically less robust, used for lighter applications where cost-efficiency is essential. Each type caters to specific packaging needs.</p></p>
