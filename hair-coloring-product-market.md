<p><p>The hair coloring product market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide a short-term color that lasts until the next wash, ideal for experimenting with bold shades. Semi-permanent dyes offer a longer-lasting option, typically fading over several washes, making them suitable for enhancing natural tones. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft, delivering long-lasting color that requires root touch-ups, providing a more enduring solution for complete color transformation.</p></p>
