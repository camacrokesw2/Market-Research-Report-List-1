<p><p>The commercial sweeping machine market is primarily divided into two types: Ride-On Sweepers and Walk Behind/Pedestrian Sweepers. Ride-On Sweepers are larger, battery or fuel-powered machines designed for efficient sweeping of expansive areas, allowing operators to sit and control the vehicle comfortably. In contrast, Walk Behind Sweepers are smaller, manual machines that require the operator to walk alongside, making them ideal for tighter spaces and smaller areas. Both types serve essential functions in maintaining cleanliness in commercial and industrial environments.</p></p>
