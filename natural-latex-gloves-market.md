<p><p>The natural latex gloves market is categorized into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable latex gloves are designed for single-use applications, offering convenience and hygiene in settings like medical facilities, food handling, and sanitation. They minimize cross-contamination and reduce infection risks. In contrast, reusable latex gloves are made for extended use, typically in industrial or household environments.
