<p><strong>The Advanced Boiling Water Reactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Cycle Steam Generation</li><li>Dual Cycle Steam Generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Boiling Water Reactors (ABWR) market is categorized into two main types based on steam generation cycles. Single Cycle Steam Generation involves the direct production of steam for turbine power, optimizing thermal efficiency but limiting output flexibility. In contrast, Dual Cycle Steam Generation utilizes a secondary loop for improved energy recovery and operational efficiency, allowing for better load management and enhanced performance under varying conditions. Both systems aim to provide safe and efficient nuclear energy solutions.</p></p>
