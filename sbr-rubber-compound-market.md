<p><strong>The SBR Rubber Compound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-SBR</li><li>S-SBR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SBR rubber compound market consists mainly of two types: Emulsion Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (E-SBR) and Solution Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (S-SBR). E-SBR is produced through an emulsion polymerization process, offering good wear resistance and aging properties, making it suitable for tires and footwear. In contrast, S-SBR is produced via solution polymerization, providing superior performance characteristics like enhanced tread wear and low rolling resistance, which are essential for high-performance tires. Both types cater to diverse applications, particularly in the automotive industry.</p></p>
