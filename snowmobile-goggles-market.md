<p><p>The snowmobile goggles market is characterized by two main types: electric heating lens goggles and non-heatable lens goggles. Electric heating lens goggles feature built-in heating elements that help prevent fogging and icing, ensuring clear visibility in cold conditions. These are ideal for extreme weather and extended use. Non-heatable lenses, on the other hand, offer traditional designs without heating features, relying on coatings and ventilation systems to minimize fogging. They are typically lighter and more affordable, catering to casual riders.</p></p>
